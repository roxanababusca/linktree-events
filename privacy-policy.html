Privacy policy

At Women Do Crypto, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you provide through our event registration form. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the data you submit. By using our registration form, you consent to the terms and practices described herein.

  1. Collection and Use of Personal Information. When you register for our event, we will collect certain personal information such as your name, phone number, and email address. This information is solely used for the purpose of reserving your spot at the event and facilitating communication regarding event confirmation, updates, or modifications. We do not disclose or sell this information to third parties.
  2. Data Security Measures. We take the security of your personal information seriously and have implemented industry-standard practices to safeguard your data. These measures include encrypted website connection, security headers, secure form handling and email providers, and more.
  3. Limitations of Internet Data Transmission. While we take every precaution to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee the full confidentiality of data transmission over the internet. It is important to note that the security practices of all parties involved in data transmission may vary. By submitting your personal information through our registration form, you acknowledge and accept this inherent risk.
  4. Consent and Concerns. By submitting your personal information through our registration form, you are giving your consent to our Privacy Policy. If you have any concerns or questions regarding the handling of your data, we encourage you to refrain from submitting your information through our forms and contact us directly to address your concerns.

At Women Do Crypto, we are committed to maintaining the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically, and any changes will be communicated on our website. We encourage you to review this policy regularly for any updates.
